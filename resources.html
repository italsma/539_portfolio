Resources

Explore some of my favorite gardening and beekeeping related resources, including tutorials, products, and inspiration.

Click on the name of each resource to visit their site.

Gardening

Gardener's World
Gardener's World is one of the best shows ever. Chock full of garden inspiration and how-to's and an excellent palette cleanser!

Floret Flowers
Floret is one of the reasons I got into gardening! Erin's gorgeous seeds and easy growing instructions make it easy to get started in any size space.

Chiltern Seeds
Based in the United Kingdom, Chiltern Seeds has a wide range of classic and unique flowers and vegetables. Plus, they deliver internationally! Excited to try a few of their varieties in the garden this year.

Swallowtail Garden Seeds
A robust seed catalogue full of interesting varieties. Swallowtail's website also includes easy-to-follow sowing and growing instructions.

Greenhouse Megastore
Meant for the more serious grower, Greenhouse Megastore has everything you need to start sowing your own seeds, including soil, seed trays, flats, and garden labels.

Beekeeping

Dadant
Dadant is my go-to, one-stop bee shop! Full of all the materials you need to get started with hive management and honey harvesting.

Ann Arbor Backyard Beekeepers
A2B2 shares informaton straight from Ann Arbor-area beekeepers. They host monthly free meetings for those curious about bees or managing their own hives.

Detroit Hives
Detroit Hives is a non-profit organization that helps transform some of Detroit's vacant lots into busy bee farms, as well as planting gardens to make them pollinator friendly.

Flow Hive
An innovatively engineered hive option, the Flow Hive helps beekeepers extract honey directly from the hive without disturbing the colony. Flow Hives also include a nifty "observation window" so you can see your bees at work without opening the hive!

"How to Harvest Honey!"
Interested to see how you get honey out of a hive and into your kitchen cupboard? YouTuber Maddie Moata's video "How to Harvest Honey!" provides a delightful overview on the whole process.

BBwear
BBwear is a UK-based beekeeping company that provides excellent protective wear for beekeepers. Tons of fun colors to pick from too!

Miscellaneous

For the things I love that don't really fit anywhere else.

Passionflower Sue
Susan McLeary is an incredible, Ann Arbor-based floral designer, specializing in wearable flower art. Her work ranges from small floral jewelry to large installations, all with a focus on beautiful flowers and sustainable materials.

The Unlikely Florist
Working out of a small studio in Venice Beach, California, the Unlikely Florist is a stellar group of passionate floral designers. Specializing in unusual flowers and long-lasting arrangements, their work makes a huge impact at events or when you just want to treat yourself. In addition to beautiful bouquets (that ship nationwide), they also sell a variety of unique tools that make perfect gifts for your gardening or design-minded friends.

Spencer
Spencer is one of Ann Arbor's best-kept dining secrets. Made with local ingredients, it is not unusual to see their chefs at the Ann Arbor Farmers' Market on a Saturday morning. Their flavors are so unique and their dishes so fresh that it often feels like you're eating straight out of the garden.

Los Poblanos
Based just outside of Albuquerque, New Mexico, Los Poblanos is a historic inn that boasts a beautiful organic farm, with acres of lavender, vegetables, and greens. It's one of the first places on my travel list post-pandemic!
